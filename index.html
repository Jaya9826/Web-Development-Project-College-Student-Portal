About Our IITM

INDORE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & MANAGMENT (IITM)

The Indore Institute of Technology & Management is one of the oldest Management Institute in Indore. Established in the year 1994 ,by The S Indore Educational and Charitable Society, Sawar(Indore).

The Institute conducts regular Post Graduate & Under Graduate programs like B.TECH,M.TECH, MBA, MCA, BBA & BCA under affiliation of D.C.R.U.S.T Murthal (Govt. University, Accredited ‘A’ Grade by NAAC) and duly approved by AICTE,New Delhi. Institute located is just opposite the railway Station and also close to Bus Stand.

The Society has been active since 1915 and has established 14 Institutions offering programmes at Secondary, Graduate and Post-Graduate levels.
